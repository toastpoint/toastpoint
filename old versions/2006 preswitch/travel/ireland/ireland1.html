Oct 5 - Writing the travelogue one paragraph at a time...

Day 1 - Dublin

Our flight on Aer Lingus was pretty painless, and we landed as the sun was coming up, a little after 6:00 a.m. Of course, to us it was about 1:00 in the morning and our butts were dragging. I had remembered from my college trip to England how jet lag in this direction was truly hellacious and it was true. We got our luggage, got a cab and found our way to the Hilton. They were very nice there, but of course, we couldn't get into our room until afternoon, so they gave us a map and off we went.
