From Toast Point and Sage

Here, browse through gardens
Of seventeen syllables
Grouped attractively

After Sage review
We combed through them all and found
Best of '96

Twelve months of haikus
From four seasons of the year
Nineteen Ninety-Six

Haikus from December
October or November
Or last September

Haikus of August
July, or even of June
Bright sunny morsels.

Before, a winter:
March, February , after
Cold January

Remember your past.
