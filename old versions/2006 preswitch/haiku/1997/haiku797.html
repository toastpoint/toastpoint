Robert R. Cobb writes 07/15/97

Imagination
seeking reality finds
some dreams can come true.

Extrapolating
known values, numerically,
assumes the unknown.

Quantifying
data statistically
proclaims evidence.

Colonization
expands universal truths
making us feel small.

Apiary hives
illustrate existing plans
assuring our space.
