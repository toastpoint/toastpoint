Limerick Listserv!

Our own Crispy, a regular contributor to the contest, is now maintaining a listserver called Partners-in-Rhyme. Partners-in-Rhyme sends you daily limericks, with word definitions, by email. To subscribe to Partners-in-Rhyme, send a mail message to majordomo@peak.org with the following line as the body of the message:

subscribe Partners-In-Rhyme
