Instructions

The Toast Point, his brain getting warm,
Has learned how to generate forms
And wishes his readers
To see who is sweeter
At writing a limerick poem. 		While making your choice, do think twice
Decide if you're naughty or nice
Then write up your entry
Submit to the sentry
And do it lots - can't beat the price!
To fill in your poem delectable
A list of first lines is selectable
But if these you don't like
Tell 'em "just take a hike"
And write your own line - that's respectable. 		Your entry will be judged by a sage
Where its relative worth will be gauged.
And the poems that glitter
Get stars for submitters
Displayed on the month's contest page!
