Special Notes

  • Each page you display should have a unique page number assigned to it in the URL (?page=n). The ?page=n suffiix doesn't actually do anything, except make the URL unique for each of your pages, even though the same image is being displayed. The reason for this is to fool browsers such as Netscape Navigator into thinking each button on each page is a separate image, forcing it to retrieve it from the server, not from it's local cache. If you don't include the ?page=n suffiix, with a unique page number for each page displaying the button, you'll only get credit for each vistor for the first time the button is displayed. Thereafter, your browser would display the local copy from its cache, and not re-retrieve the button from the server, and therefore youlose credits. It is not necessary to understand all of this, just make sure you change the page number on every page displaying the button to ensure you get full credit.
  • It is important that you display our button at http://www.webcom.com/images/hostedby.gif, not a copy of it at your site. If you make a copy of the button at your site:
    • You would incur additional traffic fees for displaying the button from your site (displaying the button from our site will not cause any additional traffic for your site)
    • You would not receive the credit for displays, since our system would not recognize the copy as the button (however you would still get credit for any click-throughs to our site).
  • We reserve the right to decline this offer to any site. Specifically, this offer is not available to "adult" sites.
  • Repeated page views of the same page by the same visitor to your site do not generate repeat discounts. Only after 1 hour can the same visitor (IP address) view the same page and generate another credit for you. However, the same visitor can generate many credits for you for viewing the button on different pages at your site within a short period of time. For example, if a visitor to your site viewed 10 different pages at your site, all containing the button, and they viewed each page several times, you would be credited for 10 "impressions" or page-views; although their total number of page views might be 30 or 40, since they only viewed 10 different pages, you recieve 10 page-view credits.. A different visitor visiting the same pages at the same time would also generate 10 impression discounts.
  • Each visitor to your site (IP address) can only generate one click credit per day, even if they click on the button several times on the same or different pages.
  • There is a one-hour delay between the time a credit is earned and the time it appears as a credit in your charges.
  • The size of the button is approximately 3k. Currently there is not a non-animated version of the button. You can create a textual 'Hosted by WebCom' link to our referral page, and receive click-thru credits, but you would not receive discounts on page views.
