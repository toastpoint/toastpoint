J. Bob Green writes a bunch 02/28/96

Old man winter dead?
No, not until spring appears.
Go back to sleep, Nature!

Love's torture, sure death
Friendship to love turns away
The heart from pity.

Misconstrued intent
Turns friends to lovers too soon
Therefore, guard intent.

Oh, the Ides of March!
Life renewed, through death's sorrow,
The fragrance of grace.

Fate, we jumped too soon
Into passion's lovers' trap
Now we pay the price.

Internet lovers
Snail-mail too slow for passion
Again, I'm alone.

Standing on the edge
Of eternity, looking
And dawn never comes.
