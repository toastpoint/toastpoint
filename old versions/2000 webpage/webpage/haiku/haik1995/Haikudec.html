Red Wood writes a bunch 12/15

After rain. Look up!
It's not the blue sky I love
But the deep shadows.

Crush a ficus leaf.
Smell of papaya. Cold rain
is suddenly warm.

Men are gathering.
On fire circle ashes
Butterfly is warm.

Fifty men and a dog
Howl in the night.
No moon.

Strong wind. A woman
Stepping through red leaves covers
Her beautiful limbs.

Wife and son in bed
Snuggle so close. Two heads have
Come from one body!
