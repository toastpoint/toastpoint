Squeaky-Clean Entries from July 1995

Sadly, we declared no winners in this category for July due to the lack of entries. Strangely enough, the naughty limerick contest had many entries...

The Entries for July

Mark Chartrand writes 7/10

The erudite Charles Grandiose
Gave answers quite often verbose.
Culling grain from the chaff
Rarely produced a laugh
And the reader became comatose

Toast Point writes 7/1

My Wumpus is covered with hair
And therefore he's never quite bare
But he's awfully sweet
So cute and so neat
That I think we make quite a pair!
