Gods and Goddesses

Singer Gods - Hop over to CD Now and do a search on Michael McDonald! They have some beautiful GIFs of him - unfortunately, they also have a proprietary notice so I can't just link to them myself.

Singer Goddesses - To visit the page of my goddess of love, try the Christine Lavin home page. Or the up-and-coming folkie who I actually know! - Susan Werner, who released her first nationally-released album, "Last of the Good Straight Girls", last year. Buy it, buy it!

Actress Goddesses - Pay your oscarial respects at the Susan Sarandon Shrine, the Emma Thompson Worship Page and the Jodie Foster Gallery! We also adore Winona Ryder. And of course, Winona's little sister from Mermaids, Christina Ricci!

Other goddesses on their way. There doesn't seem to be a Carol Burnett page...yet.

Gossip
