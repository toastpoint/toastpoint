The heat of the fireplace's flames and ambers, the glow on my eyes; hypnotizing. We camped by a small river. 8000 feet, very cold. A huge brown bear hit our camp and provided lots of excitement, but I remember the flames of the campfire, the embers. And the brook. I am sitting."
